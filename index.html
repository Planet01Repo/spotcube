How to Buy?

Click the BUY NOW button above and connect your wallet. Once connected, you'll see three options to buy LHINU tokens.

1. Buy LINHU with ETH/BNB

To buy tokens with ETH/BNB, enter how much LIHNU you want to buy, or how much ETH/BNB you want to swap, then confirm the gas cost and the transaction in your wallet.

2. Buy LINHU with USDT

To buy tokens with USDT, enter how much LIHNU you want to buy, or how much USDT you want to swap, approve the use of USDT in your wallet, then confirm the gas cost (in ETH) and the final transaction.

ECOSYSTEM

SpotCube as a multisided network platform is a business model that connects two or more groups of users who would not normally interact with each other. The platform provides a way for these users to exchange value, and it generates revenue by charging very simple fees to one or more of the groups.
A Spotic Coin payment system is a critical component of SpotCube as a multisided network platform. It allows users to easily and securely exchange value, and it helps to ensure that the platform is profitable.
